Frequently Asked Questions

What is Bride Studio?

Bride Studio is an AI-powered mobile application that allows you to virtually try on wedding dresses. By uploading a single photo and selecting a dress style, you can see a realistic preview of how you might look as a bride.

Is Bride Studio free?

You can try Bride Studio for free with limited features. Some advanced features, premium dress styles, or higher-quality generations may require a paid subscription or in-app purchase.

How does the AI work?

Bride Studio uses artificial intelligence to analyze your uploaded photo and generate a realistic wedding dress preview. The AI focuses on body proportions, lighting, and dress details to create a natural-looking result.

Are the generated images realistic?

The generated images are designed to be as realistic as possible. However, results may vary depending on photo quality, lighting, posture, and dress selection. The images are for visualization and inspiration purposes only.

Do the dresses represent real brands or designers?

No. All dresses and images generated by Bride Studio are created by AI and do not represent real products, designers, or fashion brands. Any resemblance to existing designs is purely coincidental.

Are my photos stored?

Your privacy is important to us. Uploaded photos are processed securely and are not shared publicly. Photos are stored only for the time required to generate the result and may be deleted automatically after processing.

Does Bride Studio share my data with third parties?

Bride Studio does not sell or share your personal data with third parties. Some data may be processed using trusted services (such as cloud infrastructure) strictly for providing the app’s functionality.

Can I save or download generated images?

Yes. You may save or download generated images for personal, non-commercial use. You are responsible for how you use or share downloaded images.

Can I use generated images commercially?

Generated images are intended for personal use only. Commercial use, resale, or redistribution of generated images is not permitted unless explicitly allowed.

What kind of photo should I upload?

For best results, upload a clear, well-lit photo with a neutral background. Full-body or upper-body photos work best. Avoid blurry, dark, or heavily edited images.

What happens if the result does not look good?

AI results can vary. You can try uploading a different photo, changing lighting, or selecting another dress style for better results.

Is Bride Studio safe to use?

Yes. Bride Studio is designed with user safety and privacy in mind. The app follows standard security practices and respects user data.

Can I cancel my subscription?

Yes. Subscriptions are managed through the App Store. You can cancel or manage your subscription at any time from your Apple ID settings.

Does Bride Studio work offline?

No. An internet connection is required to upload photos and generate AI-powered results.

Who owns the uploaded photos?

You retain ownership of the photos you upload. Bride Studio uses your images only temporarily to provide the service.
