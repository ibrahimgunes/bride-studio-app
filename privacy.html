Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 2025

Bride Studio ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is collected, used, and protected when you use the Bride Studio mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

Photos:
When you upload a photo to use the wedding dress try-on feature, the image is processed solely to generate your virtual bridal preview. Uploaded photos are not publicly shared.

Usage Data:
We may collect anonymous usage information such as app interactions, feature usage, and crash reports to improve app performance and stability.

Personal Information:
Bride Studio does not require you to provide personal information such as your name, address, or phone number to use the app.

2. Firebase Services

Bride Studio uses Google Firebase services to operate and improve the app. These services may collect and process data according to Google's Privacy Policy.

  • Firebase Analytics – to understand app usage and improve user experience
  • Firebase Crashlytics – to identify and fix app crashes
  • Firebase Authentication (if enabled) – to manage secure sign-in
  • Firebase Storage – to temporarily process uploaded images

Google’s Privacy Policy can be viewed here:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

3. How We Use Your Information

  • To generate AI-based wedding dress previews
  • To maintain and improve app functionality
  • To monitor app performance and prevent crashes

4. Photo Processing & Retention

Uploaded photos are used only for image generation purposes. Photos are processed securely and are deleted after processing or within a limited retention period required for service operation.

5. Data Sharing

Bride Studio does not sell, rent, or trade your personal data. Data is shared only with trusted service providers (such as Firebase) strictly for app functionality and analytics.

6. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your information using industry-standard security practices. However, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

7. Children’s Privacy

Bride Studio is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Your Rights

You may stop using the app at any time. If you have questions about your data or wish to request deletion, you may contact us.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, contact us at:

Email: support@bridestudio.app
